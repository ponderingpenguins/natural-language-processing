This study has a few limitations. First, we only evaluate on AG News, so our conclusions may not transfer to datasets with different writing styles or label structures. Second, we compare only CNN and LSTM models trained from scratch; we do not include pretrained Transformer baselines, which likely sets a higher performance ceiling. Finally, because hyperparameter tuning was done with short runs (3 epochs per configuration) and one fixed split/seed, some potentially better settings may have been missed.
